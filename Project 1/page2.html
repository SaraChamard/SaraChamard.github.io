About Tiny

Tiny is a gorgeous mahogany bay Appendix Quarter Horse -- that is, a Quarter Horse and Thoroughbred cross.
In exactly what percentage, I cannot say for sure. I do know what it means, though: she is small and quick, and also has a great butt. Other than that, she has a very refined, sometimes nearly Arabian-esque look.
Furthermore, she likes to make verytightturns and show off all her agility.
Tiny is... tiny.
I didn't name her, but it suits her (plus, it's usually bad luck to change a horse's name! Luckily for us both, her name is pretty cute). Tiny stands 14.3 hands tall, so, about 4.8' at the withers. Technically she's about an inch away from being pony-height, which is perfect, because I too, am very small.
Tiny has another name, too. She was registered with, and shown under, the name "My Magnolia Breeze", which is beautiful. Just like her.
Speaking of... Tiny had a show career!
It wasn't with me, but when Tiny was young, she was shown in Western Pleasure, I believe, with the American Quarter Horse Association. She did very well, she'll have you know! But I am not very competitive myself, and she and I are pretty content to do our own thing these days.
